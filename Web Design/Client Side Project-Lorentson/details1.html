Halo: The Master Chief Collection

Bungie's hit trilogy, Halo: Combat Evolved (2001), Halo 2 (2004), and Halo 3 (2007), along with 343 Industries' production Halo 4 (2012), are now available on Xbox One in this complete package. In addition, Halo 3: ODST (2009) and Halo: Reach (2010) have been made available as Downloadable Content. New Halo players can experience the campaign and multiplayer modes of all the classic Halo games in one place, and veteran players can feel the nostalgia of their favorite classic games all over again with the performance of the Xbox One. Available for purhcase on Xbox One or PC, or get it included with a suscription to Game Pass for Xbox or Game Pass for PC!
