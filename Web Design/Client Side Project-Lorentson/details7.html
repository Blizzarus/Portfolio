December 4th, 2020 @ 9:00 AM: LeBron James, Bugs Bunny, and Xbox Invite Fans to Create a Space Jam: A New Legacy Video Game

Microsoft will be teaming up with the producers of "Space Jam: A New Legacy." Bugs Bunny and Lebron James will be here to help Xbox gamers everywhere design their very own arcade game, inspired by the movie! Join us Live for this special event.

December 10th, 2020 @ 6:49 PM: The Game Awards 2020: Exclusive Reveals, Major Announcements, and More During a Night of Gaming Celebration!

The Game Awards is an annual ceremony to recognize remarkable talent among developers and designers in the gaming community. This year, the show will be hosted by Geoff Keighley, and guest presenters will include Keanu Reeves, Reggie Fils-Aime, Christopher Nolan, Brie Larson, and Nolan North. Join us this year to see if your favorite nominees are picked!

November 6th, 2020 @ 6:00 AM: What to Expect During the Xbox Launch Celebration

The newest generation of consoles is upon us, and coming this Novemeber, gamers will see the release of the Xbox Series X and Series S. Exciting as this is, there's lots of unaswered questions still floating around, so join us November 6th to get some answers. We'll be discussing launch plans, exclusive offers for those who pre-ordered, and where those who are waiting for launch day can get their console as soon as possible!
