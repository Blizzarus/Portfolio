Sea of Thieves

Raise the Jolley Roger and prepare to set sail, in Sea of Thieves! This game packs everything an aspiring priate could ask for, including a limitless ocean of treasures to explore, fearsome monsters to battle, countless ships to plunder, and a never-before-seen role-free system that allows each player to choose how they adventure! To come back with a bounty of gold, you'll need to work together with your crewmates to keep your ship sailing, while repelling foes, steering clear of harsh weather, and also keeping your one unpatched eye out for loot along the way! Join your friends on the high seas today in Sea of Thieves!
