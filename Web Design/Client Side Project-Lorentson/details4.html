Ori and the Blind Forest

Ori and the Blind forest is a story of tragedy, triumph, and family, set in a gorgeous hand-painted world bursting with surprises. You play as Ori, the child of the spirit tree, stolen away from home by a terrible storm. When the forest begins to die, it is up to Ori to find out what happened to cause the disaster, and put an end to it before it's too late. This Metroidvania game allows for infinite possibilities, allowing the player to freely explore as they discover new secrets and powers hidden around the forest of Nibel, all while accompanied by a brilliant orchestrated score. Experience the magic of Ori and the Blind Forest today!
