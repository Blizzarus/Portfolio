Xbox LIVE: The Xbox online service.

Xbox LIVE allows users to connect with Xbox online services to get the most out of their gaming console.

Do more with your Xbox account with a Free Xbox LIVE Silver membership!

Xbox LIVE Silver
Anyone with a Microsoft account linked to their Xbox profile can sign up for Xbox LIVE Silver for free at any time. Doing so will allow the profile to sync data like settings, saves, achievements, and more between consoles. It will also allow the use of party chat over Xbox LIVE, so you can talk to your friends while you play! Get connected with Xbox LIVE Silver now!

Get the ultimate Xbox online experience with an Xbox LIVE Gold membership!

Xbox LIVE Gold
Xbox LIVE Gold is available as an upgrade for anyone who has set up their account with an Xbox LIVE Silver membership, or as an option upon setting up your account initially. Gold allows players to freely connect to Xbox online servers and play games online, with friends or other players all around the world. Matchmaking services will group players up in a lobby so that every game pairs you with a new set of teammates and opponents. Gold also gives users access to Free games every month, up to $100 in value, at no extra charge! Check out Xbox LIVE Gold today!
